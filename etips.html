⚙️ Engineering Life Tips

Smart hacks to survive and thrive in your B.Tech journey!

Time Management

Use the Pomodoro technique while studying, and always make a weekly to-do list. Don’t wait till the deadline!

Group Projects

Choose your team wisely. Work with people who are responsible and can actually contribute.

Intern Early

Start applying for internships from the 2nd year. Use platforms like Internshala, LinkedIn, and Unstop.

Faculty Relations

Be respectful to professors. Good relations can help with attendance, projects, and recommendations.

Smart Study

Focus on PYQs, assignments, and concepts explained in class. Avoid wasting time on unnecessary syllabus.

Resume Building

Document every event, project, and skill. Update your resume every semester to track growth!
